Carrington Pointe

These clients love color. Boldly painted walls, updated furnishings and lighting add a sense of fun to the family room.


What can I say? My client wanted sparkle! So I created a conversation starter by transforming a builder-grade powder room into a statement piece.

Unable to find the perfect vanity, I created this sleek one from a Century chest. A marble backdrop wall with classic black and white geometric wallpaper, one of a kind mirror and a spectacular Schonbek chandelier makes this powder room a showstopper!


Wainscoting with a modern twist completes the main floor and stairwell. Custom drapery and beaded glass wallpaper in the living and dining rooms add warm sophistication.
