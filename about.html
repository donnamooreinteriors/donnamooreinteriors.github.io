About Donna

Donna Moore Interiors improves the way you live by delivering a beautiful home that functions specifically for you and your lifestyle.

Donna’s unique signature is her hands-on style. She understands that design is personal, and she believes that trust between designer and client is critical to successful projects.

Donna came to the world of design in a unique way, starting her career as a counselor before attaining her design degree. Those unique skills in counseling and interior design set her apart in the design industry.

Even after many years as an established designer, Donna’s masters degree in counseling still benefits her clients, giving her a specific talent for inventive problem solving, negotiating, and finding resources to complete difficult projects.
