Services

Donna Moore Interiors provides a full range of design services.

You deserve the luxury of coming home to a house that’s perfect for you. Donna Moore Interiors delivers a beautiful home that functions specifically for you and your lifestyle in this concierge-level service. She and her team carefully manage every detail from design conception to project completion, making your vision for your home come to life.

Full-service design covers every aspect of the project, whether it’s new build, renovation, or redesign. That includes working with contractors, builders, architects and trade professionals, specification services for construction materials and furnishings, sourcing, floor plans, installation and much more. We take the stress out of project management, making it a seamless process that delivers premier customer service and stunning interiors.

Whether you’re building the home of your dreams or downsizing to a new space, Donna’s expertise and knowledge will help you equip and empower your house to support you and your lifestyle for decades. Need a quick fix? Like to decorate your home yourself? Want to work on your own schedule? Do you prefer a personal experience with someone who can encourage you? Then this service is for you!

In a one-on-one session, Donna Moore will spend 3-4 hours with you in your home, sharing her expertise and answering all of your questions. She will put together a detailed summary of your time together and send you a package of notes and beautiful samples customized just for you.

And remember, it’s your day! You can work with Donna to design a new color scheme for your rooms, learn best ideas on use of spaces, floorplans, materials, furnishings, artwork, lighting, solve a design dilemma, or stage a home for sale – you set the goal and Donna will help you achieve it. Most importantly, she will show you how to use your budget wisely to create the home of your dreams.

These spots fill up fast, so book quickly. A limited number of appointments are available each month. Please contact our office to schedule your personal day with Donna!
